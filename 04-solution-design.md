# Solution Design: ZenFox

## One-Line Pitch

**ZenFox is a nature-based mental resilience programme for Irish farmers, delivered on their own land, guided by trained peers, and structured around the rhythms of the farming calendar.**

---

## What ZenFox Is

ZenFox is a hybrid service combining a mobile app with in-person, peer-led nature mindfulness sessions held outdoors on farms, at heritage sites, and in local woodlands. It reframes mental health support as "resilience training" — a practical skill for farming, not therapy for sickness.

### The Three Pillars

**1. Field Sessions (In-Person, Peer-Led)**
- 60-minute guided outdoor mindfulness sessions held fortnightly at rotating farms, local woods, or heritage landscapes.
- Led by trained "ZenFox Guides" — active or recently retired farmers who complete a 40-hour nature-based mindfulness facilitator certification.
- Structured around seasonal farming themes: calving stillness (spring), harvest focus (summer), soil rest (autumn), lambing readiness (winter).
- Groups of 6–10. No clinical language. No indoor rooms. No waiting lists.

**2. The ZenFox App (Digital Companion)**
- Daily 5–15 minute audio sessions narrated by Irish farming voices, designed for use while walking fields, feeding livestock, or sitting in the tractor cab.
- Seasonal "Resilience Trails" — 8-week guided programmes with progressive nature mindfulness practices.
- Anonymous mood check-ins (simple 1–5 scale) that detect patterns and gently suggest peer connection or escalation.
- "Fox Spotting" — a gamified nature observation journal where farmers log wildlife, weather, and landscape observations as a mindfulness anchor.
- Crisis pathway: if mood scores drop significantly, the app offers warm handoff to the IFA/Pieta helpline or local counsellor, framed as a strength, not a failure.

**3. Community Network (Peer Trust Layer)**
- Integration with IFA branches, Teagasc advisory offices, Macra na Feirme, and local co-ops as hosting partners.
- Seasonal "Field Days" — larger gatherings (20–40 people) combining practical farm skills workshops with guided nature mindfulness, normalising the practice within existing farming culture.
- Private online community (WhatsApp/Signal-based) for each local group, moderated by the ZenFox Guide.

## Who Provides It

**ZenFox Ltd** — an Irish social enterprise (Company Limited by Guarantee with charitable status pending), headquartered in Galway.

### Founding Team (Target Profile)
- **CEO/Founder:** Background in rural community development or agri-tech, lived farming experience.
- **Clinical Director:** Registered psychologist or psychotherapist with nature-based therapy certification (e.g., Nacadia® or equivalent).
- **Head of Training:** Former Teagasc advisor or Macra na Feirme leader, responsible for Guide certification programme.
- **CTO:** Mobile app development, data privacy (GDPR-compliant health data).

### Strategic Partners
- **Teagasc** — access to 1,100+ farm advisors as referral network and co-hosts.
- **IFA (Irish Farmers' Association)** — distribution channel to 72,000 members; integration with existing mental health helpline.
- **Mental Health Ireland** — clinical governance, escalation protocols, evidence framework.
- **Heritage Council / Coillte** — access to heritage landscapes and national forests for Field Sessions.
- **HSE Health & Wellbeing** — potential co-funding under Healthy Ireland framework.

## Why It Works

1. **Meets farmers where they are** — on their land, in their language, on their schedule.
2. **Peer-led trust model** — farmers listen to farmers, not therapists.
3. **Nature IS the therapy room** — leverages the strongest evidence base (wild/forest settings, Cohen's d 0.54–0.90).
4. **Non-clinical framing** — "resilience training" removes stigma; positioned alongside farm safety training.
5. **Seasonal cadence** — aligns with the farming calendar, not a clinical appointment calendar.
6. **Scalable** — Guide certification model creates distributed delivery without needing to hire therapists.
